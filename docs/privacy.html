qard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026
Summary qard does not collect, transmit, store on a server, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What qard does

qard lets you create a personal "rolodex" of social profile cards (Instagram, X, LinkedIn, TikTok, WhatsApp, and similar platforms). Each card displays a QR code that links directly to your profile on that platform, so other people can scan it and find you. There is no account, no sign-up, no cloud sync.

Data we collect

None. qard does not have a backend server. There is no place we could collect or send your data to, even if we wanted to.

Data stored on your device

The handles you enter (e.g. @yourname) and the order you arrange your cards in are saved locally in iOS's UserDefaults system, inside an App Group container shared with the qard widget extension. This data:

  • Stays on your iPhone.
  • Is not synced to iCloud (unless you enable iOS-wide encrypted device backups, which Apple controls).
  • Is not visible to qard or anyone else.
  • Is deleted immediately and permanently when you uninstall the app.

Network activity

qard makes no outgoing network requests of its own. The only "network-adjacent" actions are:

  • Opening a profile in another app: when you (or someone scanning your QR code) tap a generated link, iOS opens the relevant social media app or your default browser. That hand-off happens at the operating-system level, not through qard's code.
  • Saving a wallpaper to Photos: if you choose to export a branded wallpaper, qard writes the image to your Photos library after you grant the standard iOS permission. The image never leaves your device unless you share it yourself.

Third-party services and SDKs

qard contains no third-party software development kits. There is no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no advertising SDK. No third party receives any information about you from qard.

Tracking

qard does not engage in tracking as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. There is no IDFA access, no cross-app or cross-website tracking, and no fingerprinting.

Children

qard is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. There is nothing for qard to "knowingly collect" because qard collects nothing.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of qard introduces functionality that materially affects privacy (for example, an opt-in cloud sync feature), this page will be updated and the change date at the top will be revised. Continued use of qard after such a change constitutes acceptance.
